Apr 2023 – Jun 2023 Game Theoretic Model

A project of Game Theory course.

Leading Instructor: Pro. Zhixuan FANG, Assistant Professor at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University

Extended the algorithm in the paper Deployed armor protection: the application of a game theoretic model for security at the Los Angeles international airport

The paper uses a Bayesian Stackelberg game model with a single type of leader and multiple types of followers, and reduced it to an MILP (mixed-integer linear programming) to solve it. In this project, we extended the algorithm to solve Bayesian Stackelberg games with multiple types of leaders and followers, and examined the performance.

Result: Wrote a paper Extension of the Game Theoretic Model for Security at the International Airport
